Pig Latin

Luna Valeria

Pig Latin is a joke "language" used as a secret language in children in English-speaking countries. Its rules are simple.

  • If the word begins with a vowel (a, e, i, o, u) way must be added to the end of the word. Examples: "air" becomes "airway." "eat" becomes "eatway"
  • If the word begins with another character, take the first letter and add it to the end, adding "ay." Examples: "Python" becomes "ythonpay". "Demo" becomes "emoday" With all this in mind, develop a function which allows us to do the translation between English and Pig Latin.
